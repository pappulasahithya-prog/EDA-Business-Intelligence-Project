# Task-3: Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding

## Core KPIs

| KPI | Formula | Business Rationale |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| Total Sales | SUM(Sales) | Measures overall revenue |
| Total Profit | SUM(Profit) | Measures profitability |
| Total Orders | COUNT(Order ID) | Tracks order volume |
| Average Order Value | Total Sales / Total Orders | Shows average customer spending |
| Profit Margin | (Total Profit / Total Sales) × 100 | Indicates business efficiency |
## Deep-Dive Analysis: Segmentation Analysis

### Segment-wise Analysis
- Analyze sales and profit across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.

### Category-wise Analysis
- Compare sales and profit across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology categories.

### Region-wise Analysis
- Identify high-performing and low-performing regions.

### Sub-Category Analysis
- Find top and bottom performing sub-categories based on sales and profit.

### Customer Analysis
- Identify top customers contributing to sales and profit.
